## About Me

I'm a software engineer based in Bangalore, mostly living in Flutter and Kotlin. I like clean architecture, sensible state management, and UX that doesn't get in the way, the kind of app that just *feels* right to use.

Right now that means building **BulkBuddy**, a calorie & protein tracking app that's grown to 2,000+ active users, built end-to-end by me. I've also shipped **DailyDoze** and **BoxBreathe** on the Play Store, worked on real-world fintech features, and put together a handful of full-stack side projects along the way. I graduated from **VIT Vellore** with a B.Tech in Computer Science, and I'm still very much in "figure out how things work under the hood" mode: architecture, systems, tooling, all of it.

Off-screen: gym, trekking, and whatever sport is in season.
